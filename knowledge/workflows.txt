WORKFLOW: Lead Management

Steps:
1. Capture leads from all channels
2. Enter into CRM within 15 minutes
3. Qualify leads (Hot/Warm/Cold)
4. Assign leads within 30 minutes

Rules:
- No delay in CRM entry
- Daily tracking mandatory

---

WORKFLOW: Property Onboarding

Steps:
1. Collect property and owner details
2. Verify legal documents (title deed, EC)
3. Create listing with photos, price, and details

Rules:
- No listing without verification

---

WORKFLOW: Client Handling

Steps:
1. Understand client requirements
2. Build trust and rapport
3. Share 3–5 property options
4. Conduct site visits
5. Follow-up regularly

Rules:
- Respond within 1 hour
- No false commitments

---

WORKFLOW: Deal Closure

Steps:
1. Negotiate terms transparently
2. Collect token amount
3. Execute Agreement to Sell
4. Complete Sale Deed

Rules:
- All transactions must be documented
- Use bank payments only

---

WORKFLOW: Documentation & Compliance

Steps:
1. Maintain KYC records
2. Store agreements and transaction records
3. Ensure RERA compliance

Rules:
- Documentation mandatory
- Maintain audit trail

---

WORKFLOW: CRM & Data Management

Steps:
1. Log all interactions
2. Update lead status daily
3. Track conversions and progress

Rules:
- No offline tracking

---

WORKFLOW: Risk Management

Steps:
1. Verify all documents
2. Avoid high-risk/cash-heavy transactions
3. Maintain audit trail

---

WORKFLOW: Post-Sale Service

Steps:
1. Assist with registration
2. Support documentation
3. Collect feedback
4. Ask for referrals

---

WORKFLOW: Performance Tracking

Metrics:
- Leads handled
- Conversion rate
- Revenue
- Customer satisfaction

---

WORKFLOW: Innovation

Steps:
1. Use AI for lead scoring
2. Automate repetitive tasks
3. Improve CRM systems
