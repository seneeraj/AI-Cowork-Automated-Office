POLICIES: Legal & Compliance

- Company must comply with all Indian laws including RERA, GST, and Income Tax.
- All transactions must have written agreements; verbal agreements are invalid.
- All records (agreements, KYC, financial) must be stored for minimum 8 years.

---

POLICIES: Anti-Fraud & Ethics

- Zero tolerance for fraud, misrepresentation, forgery, or hidden commissions.
- Any fraud results in immediate termination and legal action.
- Employees must maintain honesty, transparency, and professionalism.

---

POLICIES: AML & Data Protection

- KYC (PAN, Aadhaar) mandatory for all clients.
- Suspicious transactions must be reported.
- Client data must remain confidential and secure.

---

POLICIES: HR Conduct

- No discrimination in hiring.
- Employees must follow code of conduct.
- Bribery, harassment, and misleading clients are strictly prohibited.

---

POLICIES: Financial Governance

- All payments must be via official bank channels.
- All expenses must be approved and documented.
- Regular audits must be conducted.

---

POLICIES: Technology & CRM

- CRM usage is mandatory.
- No offline deal tracking allowed.
- Role-based access control must be followed.
- Passwords must be strong and not shared.

---

POLICIES: AI & Automation

- AI must be transparent and unbiased.
- AI decisions must be reviewable.
- Data used must be clean and consent-based.

---

CORE PRINCIPLES:

- Compliance first
- Transparency always
- Documentation mandatory
- Ethics non-negotiable
